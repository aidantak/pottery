About me

Hi, I'm Veerle, a 67-year-old passionate potter from Ninove, Belgium. I have spent my life as a chef, immersing myself in the world of culinary arts. My love for ceramics and their role in enhancing the presentation of beautiful dishes inspired me to embark on a new journey in pottery-making five years ago. My love for food, traveling, the countryside, and spending time with my children and grandchildren has deeply influenced my pottery designs. I find inspiration in the world around me, including my experiences as an avid reader and outdoor enthusiast. The natural elements and warmth of life's simple pleasures are reflected in each of my creations. I believe that pottery should not be limited to mere decoration. Instead, I focus on crafting functional pieces designed to be used and enjoyed. I take great pride in knowing that my artistry brings a touch of warmth and beauty to everyday meals and gatherings. Welcome to my pottery website, where you can explore my handcrafted pieces and share in my journey. From the dinner table to the heart of the home, I invite you to let my pottery enhance your experiences and create lasting memories with your loved ones.
