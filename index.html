About

Hi, I'm Veerle, a 67-year-old passionate potter from Ninove, Belgium. After a lifetime spent as a chef, my love for ceramics and plating exquisite dishes inspired me to craft my own pottery. My creations are not just for show; they're meant to be used and enjoyed, bringing warmth and beauty to everyday life. Explore my unique, handcrafted pieces and share in my love for food, travel, and family.
